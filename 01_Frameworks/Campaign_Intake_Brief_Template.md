CONVOTIS Campaign Intake Brief

Purpose

The Campaign Intake Brief is the foundation of every campaign created within the CONVOTIS AI Campaign Engine.

The purpose of this document is to capture all relevant information required to create:

* Campaign Strategies
* ICP Definitions
* Clay Research Plans
* Messaging Frameworks
* Landing Pages
* LinkedIn Campaigns
* Digital Media Brand Campaigns
* Creative Concepts
* Video Concepts
* HubSpot Campaigns
* Optional Outbound Campaigns

The quality of the briefing directly determines the quality of the campaign strategy.

Completion Criteria

A Campaign Intake Brief is considered complete when:

* The target audience is clearly defined.
* The buying committee is understood.
* The business challenge is documented.
* The desired outcome is documented.
* The offer is defined.
* The activation channels are selected.
* Required experts are identified.
* Digital Media Brand usage has been defined.
* Expert knowledge sources have been identified.
* Existing assets are identified.
* Success metrics are defined.

Only complete briefs should move into campaign strategy development.

⸻

Key Principle

The Campaign Intake Brief should not only define what campaign will be created.

It should define:

* who the campaign is for
* which business problem is being addressed
* which expertise is required
* how expertise will be communicated
* which channels will be activated
* which conversion should be generated

The quality of the briefing determines the quality of the campaign strategy and all downstream campaign assets.

Better input creates better campaigns.
