CONVOTIS Buyer Journey Framework

Purpose

This framework defines how CONVOTIS campaigns should be structured across the entire buyer journey.

The objective is not to generate leads as quickly as possible.

The objective is to help potential buyers understand:

* their business challenges
* possible solutions
* the value of taking action

before asking them to engage with sales.

Every campaign should support all stages of the buyer journey.

⸻

Buyer Journey Philosophy

Insight Before Promotion

Buyers do not care about products.

Buyers care about:

* business outcomes
* risks
* opportunities
* operational challenges

Campaigns should therefore focus on helping buyers understand their situation before introducing a solution.

⸻

Education Before Conversion

The purpose of marketing is to create understanding.

The purpose of sales is to create commitment.

Campaigns should educate first and convert later.

⸻

Expertise-Led Media Throughout The Journey

CONVOTIS does not rely on traditional thought leaders or personal brands.

Instead, expertise is communicated through a digital media brand.

The purpose of the digital media brand is to:

* make expert knowledge scalable
* create consistency across campaigns
* reduce dependency on individual contributors
* simplify content production
* increase brand recognition
* create a recognizable communication style

Subject matter experts provide the expertise.

The digital media brand communicates the expertise.

CONVOTIS remains the primary brand.

The digital media brand acts as the communication layer between expert knowledge and the audience.

The digital media brand may formulate perspectives and observations, but always based on CONVOTIS expertise and experience.

It should never invent opinions or viewpoints that are not supported by internal expertise.

The role of the digital media brand changes throughout the buyer journey.

Awareness

Create curiosity and new perspectives.

Consideration

Provide education, insights and expert guidance.

Decision

Build confidence and explain next steps.

Digital Media Brand Principle

The objective is not to build individual thought leaders.

The objective is to build a scalable expertise platform.

Experts create knowledge.

The digital media brand communicates knowledge.

CONVOTIS owns the relationship with the audience.

The digital media brand serves as a long-term communication vehicle across all buyer journey stages.

The digital media brand may formulate perspectives and observations, but only based on expertise, experience and insights provided by CONVOTIS.

Expertise always comes first.

Personality comes second.

⸻

Role Of The Digital Media Brand

The digital media brand should function as a trusted industry communicator.

It is not:

* A personal brand
* An influencer
* A social media creator
* A fictional expert

It is:

* A communication layer
* A media format
* A recognizable content vehicle
* A scalable way to communicate expertise

The purpose is to make expert knowledge more accessible, more consistent and easier to scale across channels and campaigns.

The digital media brand should create familiarity and recognition while keeping the focus on expertise rather than personality.

⸻

Role Of Subject Matter Experts

Subject matter experts remain the source of expertise.

Their responsibility is to provide:

* Experience
* Knowledge
* Methodologies
* Customer insights
* Practical recommendations

The digital media brand translates and communicates this expertise to the audience.

Experts do not need to become public thought leaders in order for their expertise to reach the market.

⸻

Media Brand Content Principles

Every piece of content should answer at least one of the following:

* What should buyers understand?
* What risks should buyers recognize?
* What opportunities should buyers consider?
* What decisions should buyers prepare for?
* What practical insight can help them move forward?

The objective is to create clarity.

Not attention.

Key Principle

The buyer journey is not a funnel.

It is a learning process.

The role of marketing is to help buyers move from uncertainty to clarity.

The role of sales is to help buyers move from clarity to commitment.

The role of the digital media brand is to make expertise scalable throughout that journey.
