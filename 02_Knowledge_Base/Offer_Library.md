CONVOTIS Offer Library

Purpose

This document serves as the central offer intelligence library for the CONVOTIS AI Campaign Engine.

The purpose of this library is to standardize how offers are positioned, selected and activated throughout the buyer journey.

This library should be used by:

* Briefing Assistant
* Campaign Strategist
* Content Planning & Creation Agent
* Webinar Agent
* Outreach Agent
* Future Automation Workflows

The objective is to define how services and content offers should be used to generate demand, create trust and convert opportunities.

⸻

Recommendation Framework

This library serves as a recommendation framework rather than a mandatory offer catalog.

Campaign Strategists may recommend offers not explicitly listed in this library if they are relevant for:

* The target audience
* The buyer journey stage
* The campaign objective

The library should be treated as guidance rather than a limitation.

⸻

Offer Selection Principles

Right Offer, Right Stage

Offers should match the buyer journey stage.

Not every audience is ready for an assessment.

Not every audience is ready for a consultation.

The offer should align with:

* Awareness
* Consideration
* Decision

⸻

Education Before Conversion

Content offers should be used to create demand.

Commercial offers should be used to capture demand.

Offer Selection Guidelines

When selecting an offer, prioritize:

1. Buyer Journey Stage
2. Audience Relevance
3. Business Challenge
4. Desired Conversion Action
5. Internal Delivery Capacity

Do not select offers simply because they exist.

Select offers because they represent the next logical step for the audience.

⸻

Expert Knowledge Integration

Every major offer should be supported by identified subject matter experts.

Experts may contribute:

* Industry Expertise
* Technical Expertise
* Compliance Expertise
* Customer Experience
* Delivery Experience

If the Digital Media Brand (Alex) is used:

Experts remain the source of expertise.

Alex remains the communication layer.

⸻

Core Principle

The objective is not to promote an offer.

The objective is to help the audience take the next logical step in their buying journey.

The best offers reduce uncertainty, create confidence and make decision-making easier.
